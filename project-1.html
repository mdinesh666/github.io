GPS Navigation Device

Designing a distraction-free navigation tool for Swiggy and Zomato delivery partners.

The Problem

Delivery partners often struggle with phone batteries dying and distractions from other apps while navigating. The goal was to build a dedicated hardware device.

The Process

I started by selecting the ESP32 WROOM development board due to its low cost and Wi-Fi capabilities. For the display, I chose a 1.8 inch TFT screen.

The Solution

The final prototype connects to the user's phone via Bluetooth to fetch turn-by-turn data but displays only arrows and distance, reducing cognitive load.
